The Lost Tomb (盗墓笔记)

A Chinese novel series by Xu Lei (南派三叔)

About the Series

The Lost Tomb is a popular Chinese web novel series written by Xu Lei under the pen name Nanpai Sanshu. The story follows the adventures of Wu Xie, a young man who becomes involved in tomb raiding and uncovers mysterious secrets about his family's past.

The series combines elements of adventure, mystery, horror, and fantasy, set against the backdrop of Chinese history and mythology.

Main Characters

Wu Xie (吴邪)

The protagonist of the story. He is the grandson of a famous tomb raider and gets drawn into the world of adventure and mystery.

Zhang Qiling (张起灵)

Also known as "Xiao Ge" (Little Brother). He is a mysterious expert with special abilities and lost memories.

Wang Pangzi (王胖子)

Wu Xie's loyal friend and companion. He provides comic relief but is also brave and resourceful.
